4. Trader Agent Layer

5. Risk Control Agent Layer

Hard Stop-Loss / Take-Profit

Enforces strict exit rules based on predefined loss and profit limits (e.g., -5% cut loss, +10% take profit), with no override.

Risk Metric Monitoring

Real-time tracking of VaR, Beta, max drawdown, and chi-square deviation.

Email Alert System

Sends real-time email alerts on profit/loss, drawdown, or abnormal exposure to notify portfolio managers or compliance team.
